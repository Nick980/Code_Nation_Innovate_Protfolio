Favorite Hobbies

Gaming

Gaming is my favorite hobby, it helps me unwind and forget about the world around me for awhile. The game I play this most is World of Warcraft, I've been playing this for over 15 years and have spent way too much time on it but I have made friends along the way and it has helped me and my brother bond over the years.

Growing up I loved playing RTS games like Dune 2, Warcraft 1, 2, & 3 and Command and Conquer, as an adult I've fallen in love with Stellaris and have admittedly lost entire days at a time to this game, I am eagerly awaiting the next expansion pack for it releasing later this month. Below is a list of my favorite games I've played in the last year.


  • World of Warcraft - MMO


  • Stellaris - 4X RTS


  • The Witcher 3 - RPG


  • Ori and the Blind Forest - Platform Adventure


  • Ori and the Will of the Wisps - Platform Adventure


  • Dark Pictures Anthology - Interactive Drama Survival Horro

3D Printing

3D printing is another hobby of mine, I love designing my own print and printing other peoples prints from Thingiverse.com. Currently I am working on creating a power meter reading device based on the Home Assistant Glow project to help monitor my power consumption due to the increasing costs.
